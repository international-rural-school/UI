How It Works

Sign Up

If you are a board member, school admin, or just somebody that wants to help, click our login button to sign up and get started.

Find your school

School Admin can login and create, read, update and delete information on the condition of the school including general issues and the condition of equipment devices in the school (TVs, tablets, chromebooks, servers, routers, HDMI cables).

Fix

A board member can log in and mark the action as done, scheduled, or ignored, and comment with more information.
